Putting Shots Recipe

Description

CPudding shots for when you're looking for a little something special for an adult event and are tired of the traditional gelatin shots. This one's for you! It's a quick-and-easy recipe that tastes great.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup Irish cream liqueur /li>
  • 1/2 vodka/li>
  • 1 package of instant chocolate pudding mix

Steps

  1. Whisk milk, Irish cream liqueur, vodka, and instant pudding mix together in a bowl until thoroughly combined, about 2 minutes.
  2. Spoon the pudding mixture into shot glasses or disposable party shot cups. Chill until set, about 30 minutes. The shots will have the consistency of mousse.
